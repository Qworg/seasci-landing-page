The March for Science champions robustly funded and publicly communicated science as a pillar of human freedom and prosperity. We unite as a diverse, nonpartisan group to call for science that upholds the common goodand for political leaders and policy makers to enact evidence based policies in the public interest

To make the march a reality, we need support!

Expenses may include:
  • Permits
  • Road Closures
  • Portable Restrooms
  • Signs and shirts
  • Per Diems for Volunteers
  • Water for Participants
  • Fliers and other Promotional Materials

We need to raise $5,000 to make sure we can cover these costs. Join our cause, and help us make our voices heard!

Earth Supporter Shirt

With the advent of a new administration, it’s a worrying time for lovers of Science. Federal agencies have been forced to silence their social media presence. The administration has ordered that studies or data from the Environmental Protection Agency, including content on the EPA website, must be reviewed by political appointees before being released to the public. March for Science-Seattle will unite scientists and the pro-Science community, to make our voice heard!

Our goal is to promote the respect for, and use of, Science in public discourse and public policy, but we can’t do it without help.
